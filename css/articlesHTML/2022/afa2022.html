AFA - Anime Festival Asia

27th November 2022

Anime Festival Asia (AFA) is a series of anime conventions and festivals that celebrate Japanese popular culture in Southeast Asia. The first AFA event was held in Singapore in 2008, and since then it has expanded to other countries in the region, including Indonesia, Thailand, and Malaysia.

At AFA events, attendees can expect to see a variety of activities and attractions related to anime, manga, video games, and other aspects of Japanese popular culture. These can include anime screenings, cosplay competitions, musical performances by Japanese artists, merchandise booths, and appearances by popular anime voice actors and creators.

In addition to these activities, AFA events also provide a platform for companies in the anime and Japanese pop culture industry to showcase and promote their latest products and projects to a wide audience. This makes AFA a popular destination for fans and industry professionals alike, and has helped to establish it as one of the leading anime conventions in Southeast Asia.
